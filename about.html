About Us

Wenani Development Network (WeDNet) is a local non-governmental organisation (NGO) and also a social enterprise business formed and registered to uplift lives in different sectors of the society. Wenani Development Network (WeDNet) was founded in 2018. WeDNet mainstreams issues of gender, HIV /AIDS, climate change, environment and climate change management, and Rights Based Approaches (RBA) in its activities. WeDNet also does capacity building in its project initiatives, implements projects at local and national level through links with other implementing partners and also does business investments as a social enterprise to raise funds which are channelled back to the organisation for sustainability.

GOVERNANCE

Wenani Development Network (WeDNet) is governed by a Board of Trustees supported by a Secretariat comprised of the Executive Director, Director of Programs, Finance and Administrative Manager (FAM) and Monitoring and Evaluation Officer (M&E).

LEGAL STATUS

Wenani Development Network (WeDNet) is a local non-governmental organisation (NGO) registered in Malawi and a Social enterprise to raise funds which are channelled back to the organisation.
